Privacy Policy for BinaryLens

Effective date: March 20, 2026

BinaryLens is a macOS app provided by Navrion.

Data Collection

BinaryLens does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to Navrion servers.

Local Processing

LinkMap files you import are processed locally on your device. Parsed data is stored locally in the app’s local database.

Analytics and Tracking

BinaryLens does not use advertising SDKs, cross-app tracking, or third-party analytics services.

Third-Party Services

BinaryLens does not upload your imported LinkMap content to external services as part of normal operation.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support.navrion@gmail.com
